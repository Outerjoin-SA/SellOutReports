Range Gaps

Region
How to read these: the store base is every store trading in the channels you sell through (all suppliers, from actual sell-out) — so it can include whole retailers or regions where you currently have only a small presence. Use the Channel and Region filters above to focus on a comparable set. Gaps do not account for each retailer’s official ranging, so a flagged store may not be ranged to carry the line — treat these as leads to check, not a directive. “Est. upside” is a deliberately bounded estimate (a proven line extended into its biggest comparable gap stores), never a promise you can stock every gap store.
